Discover the Beauty of Travel and Aviation

Welcome to my world of travel and aviation, where I share my experiences and discoveries with you. I'm Parker Howard, a travel writer based in Sydney, Australia, with a passion for all forms of travel, from budget-friendly to luxury. With my background in commercial aviation, I bring a unique perspective to my writing and a deep appreciation for the beauty of travel and the skies above.

Join me as I explore destinations near and far, review hotels, and share my experiences flying on commercial flights and private jets. Whether you're an avid avgeek or just looking for some travel inspiration, I hope you'll find something here that speaks to your wanderlust and sense of adventure.
